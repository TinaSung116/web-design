FOUR-DAY WORK WEEK
Created for an HK4As competition, this campaign explores the idea of a four-day work week through humour and satire.
Drawing inspiration from the Cantonese phrase 「返五日做狗，貔貅都要唞」, the project uses local cultural references to visualise employee burnout and promote healthier work-life balance. 「返五日做狗，貔貅都要唞」
