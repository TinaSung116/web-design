Kraft Mac & Cheese is a contemporary restaurant brand inspired by classic American cuisine, specialising in reinventing the traditional macaroni and cheese experience. This project aims to strengthen the brand's identity and local recognition through a fresh and unconventional approach.


The advertising campaign combines traditional Chinese poetry with the familiar imagery of Kraft Mac & Cheese, creating an unexpected dialogue between classical literature and modern popular culture. By blending historical references with contemporary aesthetics, the project presents a playful yet nostalgic narrative that bridges the past and the present.
KRAFT MAC & CHEESE
This poster draws inspiration from Li Qingzhao's "Adagio of Resonance", a Song Dynasty poem that expresses loneliness, longing, and emotional unrest.
The original poem reflects feelings of melancholy and yearning. In this reinterpretation, those emotions are humorously transformed through references to uncooked Kraft Mac & Cheese. By juxtaposing profound sentiments with an everyday comfort food, the poster creates an ironic contrast while preserving the emotional atmosphere of the original work.
This poster is inspired by Bai Juyi's "A Song of Everlasting Sorrow", one of the most celebrated poems of the Tang Dynasty. The original verse conveys grief and nostalgia following the loss of a loved one. In the adapted version, the addition of Kraft Mac & Cheese introduces a playful twist, suggesting that familiar flavours and comfort food may provide solace and ease feelings of longing. The contrast between solemn literary themes and contemporary consumer culture creates a light-hearted yet thought-provoking narrative.

This poster takes inspiration from Wang Wei's poem “Red Beans.”

The original poem uses red beans as a symbol of affection and longing. In this reinterpretation, Kraft Mac & Cheese replaces the traditional symbol, transforming the dish into a metaphor for warmth, comfort, and emotional connection. Through this cross-cultural adaptation, the project explores how food can evoke memories and express sentiments that transcend time and geography.
